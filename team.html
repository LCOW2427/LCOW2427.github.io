Our Story

FryItGolden was founded in 2009 by two friends who were passionate about cooking. Geting tired of food sticking in the normals pans and not getting the crisp of fried food as expected, they decided to create a product that would help people cook the perfect fry every time. They spent 1 year researching at Lambton College's laboratory and developing the perfect frying pan, and in 2010, they launched their first product. Since then, FryItGolden has become a household name, known for its high-quality frying pans and innovative cooking technology.
